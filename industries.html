Sector 01

Government & Public Administration

Federal, state, and local government agencies rely on spatially accurate, audit-ready data systems to fulfil statutory obligations, manage public assets, and plan infrastructure. Airfree Geospatial delivers the enterprise spatial infrastructure and analytical capability that government bodies require.

How Airfree Supports Government
A
Spatial Data Infrastructure (SDI)

Design and deployment of compliant SDI frameworks meeting ICSM, ANZLIC, and ISO 19100 standards. Includes metadata management, data custodianship protocols, and interoperability with federal and state spatial data systems.

B
Land & Asset Register Systems

Geospatial asset registers for Crown land, public infrastructure, community facilities, and road networks. Structured for statutory reporting, valuation support, and long-term asset lifecycle management.

C
Planning & Compliance Mapping

Zoning, land use classification, environmental constraint mapping, and development application spatial analysis for planning authorities at all levels of government.

D
Emergency Management Spatial Systems

Operational mapping, risk zone delineation, resource deployment mapping, and recovery planning spatial support for emergency management and civil defence agencies.

Sector 02

Infrastructure & Civil Engineering

Civil infrastructure projects — roads, bridges, rail, pipelines, and major built works — generate, depend on, and produce large volumes of spatial data at every project phase. Airfree Geospatial provides the survey, photogrammetric, GIS, and data management capability to support the full infrastructure project lifecycle.

Discuss Your Requirements
Typical Engagements

Corridor Analysis

Route alignment analysis, constraint mapping, and spatial impact assessment for linear infrastructure projects including roads, pipelines, and power lines.

As-Built Documentation

UAV survey and photogrammetric capture of as-built infrastructure conditions. 3D models, orthomosaics, and point clouds delivered to survey-grade accuracy standards.

Construction Monitoring

Periodic UAV surveys and volumetric analysis for earthworks progress monitoring, stockpile measurement, and construction staging documentation.

Geospatial Data Management

Spatial data architecture for large infrastructure projects — integrating survey data, engineering drawings, environmental data, and asset information into a managed GIS environment.

Sector 03

Utilities & Energy Networks

Electricity distribution networks, gas transmission systems, water and wastewater infrastructure, and telecommunications networks all depend on accurate, current, and functionally complete spatial asset records. Airfree Geospatial provides the GIS architecture and data management systems that underpin network operations and regulatory compliance.

Support Areas

Network Asset Register GIS

Structured geospatial asset registries for electricity poles, conductors, substations, pipelines, valves, meters, and telecommunications infrastructure. Integrated with SCADA, ERP, and AM systems.

02
Network Tracing & Connectivity

Topologically correct network models enabling upstream/downstream tracing, isolation zone identification, and outage impact analysis across distribution and transmission infrastructure.

03
Regulatory Compliance Mapping

Spatial reporting for AER, AEMC, ESC, and state regulatory bodies. Safety management maps, easement compliance, and vegetation encroachment detection using remote sensing.
