Drone, Photogrammetry & 3D Spatial Engineering

Overview

High-Resolution Spatial Data from Aerial Platforms

Unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) survey operations combined with photogrammetric processing workflows produce spatial data products of exceptional geometric accuracy and resolution. Where traditional survey methods are time-consuming or physically hazardous, UAV-derived products deliver equivalent or superior spatial information at a fraction of the time and cost.

Airfree Geospatial manages the complete UAV survey workflow — from flight planning and ground control point (GCP) establishment through to photogrammetric processing, quality assessment, and delivery of georeferenced spatial products in formats suitable for GIS integration, engineering analysis, and regulatory reporting.

Problem Context

Infrastructure owners, construction managers, and environmental assessors frequently require spatial data at ground sample distances (GSD) and accuracy levels that satellite imagery cannot provide — yet traditional manned aerial survey is cost-prohibitive for site-scale applications.

UAV photogrammetry fills this gap: providing centimetre-level GSD imagery, survey-grade 3D point clouds, and high-accuracy terrain models at costs and mobilisation times suitable for project-level deployment. The result is spatial data previously only achievable through expensive ground survey or manned aerial campaigns.

Solutions Provided

What We Deliver Under This Service

UAV Flight Operations

Mission planning, CASA-compliant flight operations, GCP establishment and RTK/PPK positioning, and flight data quality control. Nadir and oblique capture modes for structure inspection and terrain survey applications.

Orthomosaic Production

Georeferenced orthomosaic generation at ground sample distances from 1cm to 10cm depending on flight altitude and sensor. GDA2020 projected output in GeoTIFF format with embedded coordinate reference system metadata.

3D Point Cloud Generation

Dense multi-view stereo reconstruction producing classified LAS/LAZ point clouds. Ground point filtering, classification (ASPRS), noise removal, and delivery at specified point densities for engineering and construction applications.

Digital Terrain & Surface Models

Digital Surface Model (DSM) and bare-earth Digital Terrain Model (DTM) production from UAV point cloud data. Contour generation, slope and aspect derivatives, and hydrological modelling surfaces for civil engineering design.

3D Mesh Modelling

Textured 3D mesh models of structures, terrain, and built environments. OBJ, FBX, and 3D Tiles output formats for integration with BIM platforms, digital twin environments, and web-based 3D viewers (Cesium JS).

Volumetric Analysis

Cut and fill volume calculation from multi-epoch DSM differencing. Stockpile volume measurement, earthworks progress quantification, and pit/void volume reporting to engineering survey accuracy standards (±2% of machine-measured volumes).

Delivery Workflow

UAV Survey Process

01
Scope & Flight Planning

Survey area definition, target GSD and accuracy specification, flight planning (overlap, altitude, pattern), NOTAM application, CASA airspace authorisation, and site hazard assessment.

02
Ground Control & Survey

GCP placement and GNSS survey to GDA2020 (MGA2020). RTK or PPK positioning depending on accuracy requirements. Check point establishment for independent accuracy verification.

03
UAV Data Capture

Autonomous flight operations with real-time monitoring. Image quality inspection in field. Multi-pass acquisition for oblique coverage where required for structure inspection applications.

04
Photogrammetric Processing

Structure from Motion (SfM) and Multi-View Stereo (MVS) processing. GCP bundle adjustment, point cloud densification, orthomosaic and DSM generation. Internal accuracy report review.

05
QA & Delivery

Independent check point validation, RMSE reporting against specification, product QC, and delivery in agreed formats with comprehensive accuracy and metadata report.
