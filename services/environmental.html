Service Overview

Landscape Intelligence at Scale

AIRFREE GEOSPATIAL delivers geospatial intelligence pipelines that translate raw satellite imagery, field survey data, and airborne sensor collections into quantified environmental insight. Our workflows are engineered for repeatability, transparency, and regulatory defensibility.

We integrate multi-source earth observation data — Sentinel-2, Landsat-9, hyperspectral sensors, UAV-acquired orthomosaics, and LiDAR point clouds — into analysis pipelines capable of producing carbon inventories, habitat condition assessments, and biodiversity layer stacks compliant with Australian and international methodological standards.

Outputs are designed for submission to regulatory agencies, environmental market registries, and strategic land management authorities. Every analysis is traceable to documented methodology, source data provenance, and spatial accuracy metadata.

Problem Context

Where Conventional Approaches Fall Short

CHALLENGE 01

Carbon accounting and biodiversity assessments frequently rely on ground-truth data alone — labour-intensive, time-consuming, and spatially incomplete across large or remote areas.

CHALLENGE 02

Regulatory frameworks (ERF, ACCU scheme, State EPBC offsets) require quantified, spatially explicit evidence — outputs that anecdotal or manual survey methods cannot reproducibly generate.

CHALLENGE 03

Ecosystem change detection across multi-year temporal windows demands consistent spectral normalisation, cloud masking, and band arithmetic that conventional desktop workflows cannot efficiently execute at catchment or landscape scale.

CHALLENGE 04

Carbon market credibility requires methodological transparency and data lineage documentation — requirements that ad hoc remote sensing analyses rarely satisfy.

Analytical Workflow

From Data Acquisition to Regulatory Output

01

Scope & Methodology Definition

Project area definition, regulatory pathway identification (ERF, EPBC, State offset), applicable methodological framework selection, and baseline period confirmation.

02

Data Acquisition & Pre-Processing

Satellite image acquisition (Sentinel-2, Landsat-9, PlanetScope), atmospheric correction (Sen2Cor, ACOLITE), cloud masking, spectral normalisation, and compositing for target temporal windows.

03

Classification & Index Derivation

OBIA / machine learning classification (Random Forest, SVM, U-Net), vegetation index stack generation, biomass model application, and habitat condition scoring across the analysis extent.

04

Accuracy Assessment & Validation

Ground-truth validation using stratified random sampling, confusion matrix generation, overall accuracy and Kappa coefficient reporting. Field data reconciliation and uncertainty quantification.

05

Report, Spatial Package & Registry Submission

Technical report with documented methodology, ISO 19115 metadata, spatial data package (GeoPackage/Shapefile/GeoTIFF), and evidence portfolio formatted for regulatory submission or carbon registry lodgement.

  .filterBounds(aoi)
  .filterDate('2023-01-01','2023-12-31')
  .filter(ee.Filter.lt('CLOUDY_PIXEL_PERCENTAGE', 10))
  .map(maskS2clouds);

var composite = s2.median().clip(aoi);
var ndvi = composite.normalizedDifference(['B8','B4'])
           .rename('NDVI');
var nbr  = composite.normalizedDifference(['B8','B12'])
           .rename('NBR');
